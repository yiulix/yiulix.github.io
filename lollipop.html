Lollipop

This is a casual rhythm game made for Global Game Jam 2018 by our 5 students group. The theme is "Transmission".

  • Demo
  • Gameplay
  • Credits

Goal:

  • Move the bubble from left to right to the next level.

Mechanics:

  • Using "Q", "W", "E", "R", "U", "I", "O", "P" to control the eight lollipops.
  • Protecting the bubble from falling into water.
